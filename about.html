About GEOPOLIS Society

GEOPOLIS Society is a student-led academic platform at the Department of Politics & International Relations, Pondicherry University, focused on geopolitics, governance, and public policy research.

Vision

To build a strong academic culture of research, critical thinking, and informed debate in geopolitics and public policy.

Mission

  • Promote research writing among students
  • Encourage discussion on global political issues
  • Publish student-led academic work
  • Develop policy understanding and analytical skills

What We Do

  • Research Articles & Journal Publication
  • Policy Brief Writing
  • Monthly Discussions & Debates
  • Guest Lectures & Academic Events

Why GEOPOLIS?

GEOPOLIS provides a platform for students to engage with real-world political developments, enhance their academic profile, and build skills useful for civil services and research careers.
